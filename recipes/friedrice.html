Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a versatile and popular dish originating from China, but it is enjoyed in various forms across many Asian countries and worldwide. It is made by stir-frying pre-cooked rice with a variety of ingredients such as vegetables, eggs, meat, and/or seafood. The dish is seasoned with soy sauce, garlic, and other spices to enhance its flavor.

ingredients

  • chilli peppers
  • tomatoes
  • mushrooms
  • cooked beans
  • tortilla
  • Onions

Steps To Follow:

  1. Step:Preparation: Cook the rice a day ahead and let it cool to room temperature. This ensures that the grains are separate and not sticky.
  2. Step:Stir-Frying: Heat oil in a large pan or wok. Add minced garlic and ginger (if using), followed by diced vegetables and protein. Stir-fry until they are almost cooked.
  3. Step:Add Rice: Push the vegetables and protein to one side of the pan and add the cooked rice to the empty side. Break up any clumps of rice with a spatula and mix well with the vegetables and protein.
  4. Step:Seasoning: Pour soy sauce over the rice and mix well. Add salt, pepper, or other seasonings to taste.
  5. Step:Final Touch: Add chopped green onions or cilantro for garnish and extra flavor.
