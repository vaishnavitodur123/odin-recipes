Description

A burrito is a mexican dish which is also knows as taco or tortilla filled with Yummy ingredients let us know about the ingredients

  • chilli peppers

Steps To Follow:

  1. Step: Prepare Ingredients: Cook rice according to package instructions and season with salt and lime juice. Cook beans until they are tender. If using canned beans, drain and rinse them. Grill or saut√© your choice of vegetables until they are tender and slightly charred. Cook your choice of meat or protein and shred or chop it into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Step:Warm Tortillas: Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Warm each tortilla for about 30 seconds on each side until they are soft and pliable.
  3. Step:Assemble Burrito: Lay out a tortilla on a flat surface. Spoon a generous portion of rice onto the center of the tortilla. Add a layer of beans on top of the rice. Place your choice of grilled vegetables and meat or protein on top. Sprinkle with cheese, if using. Add a dollop of salsa, guacamole, or sour cream. Top with lettuce or spinach for added freshness.
  4. Step:Fold Burrito: Fold the bottom edge of the tortilla over the filling. Fold in the sides. Roll the burrito away from you, keeping it tight to enclose the filling.
  5. Step:Serve: Place the burrito seam-side down on a plate. You can serve it whole or cut it in half diagonally for easier eating. Serve with extra salsa, guacamole, or sour cream on the side.
